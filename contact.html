Who Are We

We are a special team dedicated to ensuring the safety and well-being of turtles in Sri Lanka. Our mission is to protect and conserve these magnificent creatures through our collective efforts. Working in collaboration with three renowned turtle hatcheries - Hikkaduwa, Bentota, and Kosgoda - we strive to make a positive impact on the conservation of sea turtles.
