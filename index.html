Turtle Hatcheries Around SriLanka

Turtle hatcheries in Sri Lanka: Conservation centers aimed at protecting and conserving endangered sea turtle species through educating and releasing hatchlings into the sea.

Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery, located in the southern coastal town of Hikkaduwa, is dedicated to conserving and protecting sea turtles. Visitors can observe and learn about the various species of sea turtles found in Sri Lanka, and also have the opportunity to release baby turtles into the ocean.

Kosgoda Turtle
Conservation Project

The Kosgoda Turtle Conservation Project, situated in the village of Kosgoda, is another hatchery focused on the conservation of sea turtles. The hatchery operates as a turtle sanctuary, providing a safe haven for injured or sick turtles to recover. Visitors can also participate in the release of baby turtles into the sea.

Bentota Turtle Hatchery

The Bentota Turtle Hatchery, located in the coastal town of Bentota, is committed to protecting sea turtles through education and conservation efforts. In addition to observing and learning about sea turtles, visitors can also participate in the hatchery's turtle release program and release baby turtles into the ocean.
